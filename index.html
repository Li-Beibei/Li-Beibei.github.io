Beibei Li is currently an associate professor at the College of Cybersecurity, Sichuan University, P.R. China. He received his B.E. degree (awarded Outstanding Graduate) in communication engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China, in 2014 and his Ph.D. degree (awarded Full Research Scholarship) from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2019. He was invited as a short-term visiting researcher at the Canadian Institute for Cybersecurity, University of New Brunswick, Canada and also the research group of NEtworked Sensing and Control (NESC), Zhejiang University, P.R. China. He is now serving as a publication co-chair for PST 2020 and a TPC member for ML4CS 2020, respectively.

Research Interests

My research interests span several areas in the security and privacy issues on cyber-physical systems (CPS), with a focus on:
  • Smart grid security
  • Industrial control system security
  • Intrusion detection techniques
  • Applied cryptography
  • Artificial immunology / Artificial intelligence
  • Big data security and privacy
    • You are warmly welcomed to contact me, if you have any interest in my research.
