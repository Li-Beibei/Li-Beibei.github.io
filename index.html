Beibei Li, IEEE Senior Member, is currently a Full Professor (Doctoral Advisor) with the School of Cyber Science and Engineering, P.R. China. He received his B.E. degree (awarded Outstanding Graduate) in communication engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, China, in July 2014 and his Ph.D. degree (awarded Full Research Scholarship) from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in March 2019. He was invited as a short-term visiting researcher at the Canadian Institute for Cybersecurity, University of New Brunswick, Canada and also the research group of NEtworked Sensing and Control (NESC), Zhejiang University, P.R. China. He is named in the World's Top 2% of Scientists List 2024.

He is now serving as an Associate Editor for the Journal of Information Processing Systems, a Junior Editor for the Complex Engineering Systems, a Youth Editor for the Journal of Information and Intelligence, a Leading Guest Editor for the Special Issue "IoT Technology and Information Security" of Applied Sciences, a Guest Editor for the Special Issue "Advanced Battery Management Technology in Electric Vehicles: Present Status and Future Trends" of Machines, and a TPC Member for the IEEE GLOBECOM 2024, GIIS 2025.

Research Interests

Dr. Li's research interests span several areas in security and privacy issues on Internet of Things (IoT) and Cyber-Physical Systems (CPSs), with a focus on:
  • Industrial Control System Security/Smart Grid Security/Cyber-Physical Security
  • Internet of Things Security/Industrial Internet of Things Security
  • Data Security/Privacy Preservation/Applied Cryptography
  • Artificial Immune/Artificial Intelligence Security
